MWR — Money-Weighted Return

Enter your overall portfolio cash flows to calculate your annualised money-weighted return (IRR).

Metric definitions
ROI Return on Investment — (Market Value − Cost Basis) ÷ Cost Basis × 100 —
ROE Return on Equity — Net Income ÷ Shareholders' Equity × 100 —
MWR Money-Weighted Return — annualised IRR of your cash flows —
SWR Safe Withdrawal Rate — 4% of portfolio value per year (the "4% rule") —
